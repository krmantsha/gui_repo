Locators¶

Locators receive phase picks from modules such as scautoloc, screloc or scolv for locating seismic or non-seismic sources. The solutions may include source time and location with or without uncertainties. They are used to form new origins which can be treated further.

SeisComP ships with built-in locators:

  • FixedHypocenter (FH)

  • Hypo71

  • iLoc

  • LOCSAT, the default locator in scautoloc and scolv

  • NonLinLoc

  • StdLoc

and a none built-in locator:

  • LocExt.

While the built-in locators are well defined and documented, any other external locator routine can be added to SeisComP by configuration of the locator LocExt and custom scripts. LOCSAT and FixedHypocenter are native to SeisComP. All other locators are implemented as plugins. A plugin needs to be added to the list of loaded plugins by configuration of the global parameter plugins for making the corresponding locator available to SeisComP applications.
